Styles

All styles of ballet have roots tracing back to the French and Italian renaissance. From there it has branched out into many different styles. Examples of these styles are Balanchine, Bournonville, Cecchetti, Vaganova and the Royal Ballet.

Each of these styles have different characteristics that make them unique. While some differences might be as small as calling it something different or as big as a technical difference.

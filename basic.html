Basics

The average ballet classes have two distinct parts, there is a part at the barre and a part in the center. Class starts at the barre and slowly picks up pace to advance to the fun, but hard tricks. The part in the barre is the preparation for center and center is the preparation for a performance.

Young students start dancing in flat shoes while the more advanced students slowly graduate to pointe work. Going on pointe takes years of dedication and training, and is often very painful.

Hierarchy

Like everything in the world, the ballet world has a hierarchy. Everyone starts as a student. When joining a company they start at the Corps de ballet. From there they can become a demi-soloist, then a soloist and sometimes even a principal dancer.

Moving up in the ranks in a company requires tremendous perseverance and dedication. It is often preceded by a feature in a higher role
